Lifestyles Owner: Miranda FunkLocation: 445 Salisbury Rd, Toronto, ON, Canada After graduating from University of Ottawa, with a degree in Nutrition, Miranda, 30,  moved to Toronto with an aim to be a gym trainer or if lucky find a job as a nutritionist. Miranda has always been very fond of fitness and health from an early age. At the age of 23, she suffered from minor levels of hypothyroidism, which makes her put on weight easily. She set a goal to change her eating habits and work towards a healthier lifestyle. Her determination led her to become a gym trainer soon by the age of 25. She decided to pursue her career in fitness and nutrition related fields, which encouraged her to further study nutrition. She strongly believed in helping others who also suffered from similar weight issues and hence opened Lifestyles. Lifestyles targeted towards helping local consumers also suffering from weight issues. It runs on weekly subscription, where every week the consumers would pick up a package of either pre cooked meals or ingredients for meals that can cook themselves. The store also holds a regular grocery store layout where one can just walk in and shop.
