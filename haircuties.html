HairCutiesOwner: Brenda OrvileLocation: 445 Ellington Ave, Toronto, ON, Canada  Brenda, 66, has always been very fussy about children’s hair and their neatness in her family. Being from a joint family, most of the mothers in the family would send their little daughters, sons and toddlers to Brenda’s before school time for her to do their hair as quickly and as neatly as possible. Amongst the family one of Brenda’s distant relative, Cathy saw this and decided to open a haircut salon specifically for young children with the owner as Brenda. Cathy believes that Brenda has an aura about her that makes the children listen to her during haircuts. Brenda often visits the salon to play with the young children and see the work. Brenda says that “Seeing the children smile makes her happy.”
